Random Dot Stereogram





Instructions

  • You will need red/cyan 3D paper glasses like this one.
  • Stare at the dots. No illusion yet.
  • Press left/right buttons to move some of the dots.
  • The dots that are moved will appear to be at different depth than others.

How the Depth Illusion Works

To generate a 3D percept we need to project the two different pictures to different eyes. Thus, the viewer wears red/cyan glasses. The eye with the red lens will see the cyan image and vice versa. Somehow these different horizontally shifted images are fused and form a 3D percept.
